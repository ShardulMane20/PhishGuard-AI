        Phishing Protection Tips

        Check the URL

        Look for misspellings, extra characters, or suspicious domains before clicking any link

        Verify HTTPS

        Ensure sensitive sites use HTTPS, but remember: phishing sites can have SSL too

        Beware of Emails

        Never click links in unexpected emails, especially those claiming urgency or threats
